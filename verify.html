How Verification Works

Every approved agent and driver goes through a rigorous vetting process.

✓

Step 1: Application

The agent or driver submits their application with all required documents and personal information.

🔍

✅

Step 3: Presidential Sign-Off

The Office of the President gives final approval, generating an official SUG ID number.
